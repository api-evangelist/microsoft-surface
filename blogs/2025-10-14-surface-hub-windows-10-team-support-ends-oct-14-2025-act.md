On October 14, 2025, support for Windows 10 Team Edition will end . On the same date, the Microsoft Teams App on Windows 10 Team will also stop functioning, devices will no longer be able to log into Microsoft Teams. This means any Surface Hub 2S devices that remain on Windows 10 Team after that date will lose the ability to join Teams meetings and will no longer receive critical security updates.
